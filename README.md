# 🎉 Soc Ops

### Break the ice with social bingo—the most fun way to meet people!

**Soc Ops** is an interactive social bingo game designed for in-person mixers, team events, and networking sessions. Find people who match the prompts, mark your squares, and race to get 5 in a row. It's icebreaking made easy, engaging, and addictive!

---

## ✨ Why Soc Ops?

- **🎯 Instant Engagement** — No awkward small talk. Everyone has a mission!
- **🎮 Gamified Networking** — Turn meeting new people into a fun competition
- **📱 Mobile-Friendly** — Works on any device, no app download needed
- **⚡ Zero Setup** — Just share the link and play
- **🎨 Fully Customizable** — Adapt questions to any event or audience

---

## 🚀 Quick Start

Get your game running in 3 simple steps:

```bash
# 1. Install dependencies
npm install

# 2. Start development server
npm run dev

# 3. Open http://localhost:5173 in your browser
```

That's it! Your social bingo game is ready to play. 🎊

---

## 🎲 How It Works

1. **Open the game** on your phone or laptop
2. **Read a bingo square** (e.g., "Find someone who speaks 3+ languages")
3. **Meet people** and ask them questions
4. **Mark your square** when you find a match
5. **Get 5 in a row** to win! (horizontal, vertical, or diagonal)

The free space in the center is already marked to give you a head start!
